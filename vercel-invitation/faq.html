FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you might wonder before the big day.

How do I RSVP?

Use the QR code or personal link on your invitation. If you need help, please contact the couple directly.

Is RSVP required?

Yes, RSVP is strictly required and seats are reserved in advance. Please respond as soon as possible so final headcount can be prepared.

Can I bring a plus one?

No. This event is strictly by invitation only and limited to listed guests.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony starts at 2:00 PM sharp at St. John Marie Vianney Parish Church. Please arrive early.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

The ceremony is unplugged. Please enjoy the moment and leave coverage to the photo/video team.

Are kids allowed?

Only children specifically requested by the couple are included.
